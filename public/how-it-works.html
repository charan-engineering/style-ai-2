How StyleAI Works

A deep dive into the technology and processes that power your personal digital stylist.

Frontend Stack

  • ✦ HTML5 & CSS3: Custom layouts and glassmorphism.
  • ✦ Tailwind CSS: High-performance utility styling.
  • ✦ Vanilla JS: Fast, interactive UI without framework overhead.

Backend Stack

  • ✦ Node.js & Express: The core API engine.
  • ✦ Gemini 2.0 Flash: State-of-the-art vision & logic AI.
  • ✦ Jimp: Pure-JS image processing for stability.

The Analytical Process

01

Multi-Input Processing

The app captures your photo and occasion details. The occasion acts as a "style prompt" that steers the AI's creative logic.

02

Vision Analysis

Jimp resizes the image and samples your skin tone by analyzing pixel clusters at the center of the photo to categorize your undertone.

03

Gemini Intelligence

Google Gemini analyzes your physical traits and occasion to generate a structured JSON profile containing color palettes and outfit logic.

04

Product Synthesis

Suggested garments are transformed into functional Google Shopping links, providing a direct bridge from AI advice to real shopping.
